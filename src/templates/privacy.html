Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 17, 2026

Patagon Consulting Inc. ("we", "our", or "us") operates the Patagon Accounting application. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our QuickBooks integration service.

1. Information We Collect

When you connect your QuickBooks account to our application, we may access:

  • Company information (name, address, contact details)
  • Customer and vendor data
  • Invoice and payment information
  • Chart of accounts and financial data

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide and maintain our accounting integration services
  • Display your QuickBooks data within our application
  • Create and manage vendors, invoices, and bills on your behalf
  • Improve our services and user experience

3. Data Storage and Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data:

  • All data is transmitted using SSL/TLS encryption
  • OAuth 2.0 authentication with QuickBooks
  • Access tokens are stored securely and refreshed automatically
  • We do not store your QuickBooks login credentials

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your information to third parties. Your data is only shared with:

  • Intuit/QuickBooks (as required for the integration to function)
  • Service providers necessary to operate our application

5. Data Retention

We retain your data only as long as necessary to provide our services. You may request deletion of your data at any time by disconnecting your QuickBooks account or contacting us.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Disconnect your QuickBooks account at any time

7. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at:

Patagon Consulting Inc.
Email: info@patagonusa.com

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.
